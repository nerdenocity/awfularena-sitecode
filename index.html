Game settings:



  • Melee crits only
  • Sprays enabled
  • No random bullet spread
  • Default spawn time
  • Respawn time disabled when player count is under 7 people
  • Vote command for restricting everyone to stock weapons only (until the next mapchange)
  • Clipless Rocket Jumpers
  • NO-SCOPE WEDNESDAYS: Snipers are banned on Wednesdays
  • FUNDAYS: Fun maps added to map rotation on Fridays and Sundays
  • Cabers restock with ammo packs
  • "Pregame mayhem" while waiting for players. (Friendly fire for goofing around before the round starts)
